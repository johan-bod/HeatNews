## Where HeatStory Fits

### Unique differentiators
1. **Geographic heat-scored news visualization** — no consumer product does this
2. **Source credibility tiers** — 6-tier system weighting sources by editorial standards (Ground News shows bias; HeatStory shows trustworthiness)
3. **Multi-scale architecture** — local to global in one interface
4. **Emerging story detection** via hyperlocal convergence

### Market gap
Between free tools (GDELT, World Monitor) and enterprise platforms (Meltwater at $25K/yr), there is no geographic news intelligence product at $50-$200/mo.

### Competitive risks
- **World Monitor**: free, open source, same Globe.gl tech, 2M users, 36+ data layers. Defense: editorial curation, credibility weighting, journalist-oriented UX.
- **Ground News**: validated the "alternative lens" category at consumer prices. Complementary (bias vs geography), not competitive.

## Suggested Tier Structure

| Tier | Price | Target | Key Features |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | Lead generation | Globe + feed, 4hr refresh, last 24hrs, soft gate (~10 visits/mo) |
| **Pro** | $9-15/mo or $89-149/yr | Individual journalists, freelancers, researchers | 1hr refresh, investigation tool, credibility tiers, alerts, export |
| **Newsroom** | $49-99/mo/seat (min 3) | Newsrooms, research teams | Real-time refresh, API access, team dashboards, analytics |
| **Enterprise** | $500+/mo (custom) | Wire services, large newsrooms, think tanks | Full API, custom sources, white-label, SLA |
